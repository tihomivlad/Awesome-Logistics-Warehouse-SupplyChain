## Books & Textbooks

### Foundational Texts

- **Supply Chain Management: Strategy, Planning, and Operation** by Sunil Chopra and Peter Meindl - Comprehensive textbook covering all aspects of supply chain management
- **Operations and Supply Chain Management** by F. Robert Jacobs and Richard Chase - Core operations management principles
- **The Goal: A Process of Ongoing Improvement** by Eliyahu M. Goldratt - Classic novel on Theory of Constraints
- **Logistics and Supply Chain Management** by Martin Christopher - Strategic perspective on logistics
- **Purchasing and Supply Chain Management** by Robert Monczka, Robert Handfield, Larry Giunipero, and James Patterson - Comprehensive procurement guide

### Warehouse & Distribution

- **World-Class Warehousing and Material Handling** by Edward Frazelle - Practical warehouse operations guide
- **The Warehouse Management Handbook** by James Tompkins and Jerry Smith - Best practices and systems design
- **Lean Warehousing** by Ken Ackerman - Applying lean principles to warehouse operations
- **Inventory Management Explained** by David Piasecki - Practical inventory control techniques

### Lean & Continuous Improvement

- **The Toyota Way** by Jeffrey Liker - Toyota Production System principles
- **Lean Thinking** by James Womack and Daniel Jones - Lean methodology fundamentals
- **The Lean Six Sigma Pocket Toolbook** by Michael George, John Maxey, David Rowlands, and Malcolm Upton - Quick reference guide
- **This Is Lean** by Niklas Modig and Pär Åhlström - Resolving the efficiency paradox

### Strategic & Advanced Topics

- **Supply Chain Risk Management** by Robert Handfield and Kevin McCormack - Identifying and mitigating supply chain risks
- **The New Supply Chain Agenda** by Reuben Slone, Paul Dittmann, and John Mentzer - Strategic transformation
- **Demand-Driven Forecasting** by Charles Chase - Modern forecasting approaches
- **The Handbook of Logistics and Distribution Management** by Alan Rushton, Phil Croucher, and Peter Baker - Comprehensive reference text
- **Supply Chain Science** by Wallace Hopp - Quantitative models and analysis

### Industry-Specific

- **Food Supply Chain Management** by Michael Bourlakis and Paul Weightman - Food and beverage logistics
- **Global Logistics and Supply Chain Management** by John Mangan and Chandra Lalwani - International trade perspective
- **Humanitarian Logistics** by Martin Christopher and Peter Tatham - Relief supply chain management

### Technology & Innovation

- **Supply Chain Management Best Practices** by David Blanchard - Technology implementation cases
- **Supply Chain 4.0** by Knut Alicke, Daniel Rexhausen, and Andreas Seyfert - Digital transformation
- **The Definitive Guide to Transportation** by CSCMP and Appelbaum - Transportation management principles

### Practical Guides

- **The Essential Guide to Supply Chain Management** by Joe Sweeney - Quick reference for practitioners
- **Warehouse & Distribution Science** by John Bartholdi and Steven Hackman - Engineering approach to warehouse design
- **The Supply Chain Manager's Problem-Solver** by Charles Poirier - Tactical solutions for common challenges
